Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a property?

Browse listings, select your dates and guests, then click "Book Now". You'll receive a confirmation email within minutes.

Is my payment secure?

Yes. All payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels. We never store your credit card details.

How do I become a host?

Register with a Host account, fill in your property details, upload photos, and set your availability. Our team will review and approve within 24 hours.

What is the cancellation policy?

Each property has its own cancellation policy. You can view the policy on the listing page before booking.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team by email at support@vacationvibe.co.il or by phone at 1-800-VIBE (Sun–Thu, 9am–6pm).
